═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  AI vs HUMAN GAME - CONTENT PREPARATION GUIDE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

To make the game fully functional, you need to add content files
to the appropriate folders. The game requires 10 pairs of content
for each category (40 pairs total).

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
📝 TEXT CONTENT (Required: 10 pairs)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Location: public/content/text/

Files needed:
  ✓ human_1.txt to human_10.txt (PROVIDED: 1-3, NEED: 4-10)
  ✓ ai_1.txt to ai_10.txt (PROVIDED: 1-3, NEED: 4-10)

Tips for creating text content:
  • Human text: Use natural language, personal stories, emotions
  • AI text: Can be more formal, structured, or obviously generated
  • Keep length similar (150-300 words recommended)
  • Topics: stories, poems, reviews, descriptions, dialogues

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
🖼️ IMAGE CONTENT (Required: 10 pairs)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Location: public/content/images/

Files needed:
  • human_1.jpg to human_10.jpg
  • ai_1.jpg to ai_10.jpg

Supported formats: .jpg, .png, .webp

Tips for sourcing images:
  • Human images: Photos, hand-drawn art, paintings, designs
  • AI images: Use DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, etc.
  • Keep similar themes/subjects for fair comparison
  • Recommended size: 800x800 to 1920x1080 pixels

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
🎵 AUDIO CONTENT (Required: 10 pairs)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Location: public/content/audio/

Files needed:
  • human_1.mp3 to human_10.mp3
  • ai_1.mp3 to ai_10.mp3

Supported formats: .mp3, .wav

Tips for audio content:
  • Human audio: Real music, voice recordings, natural sounds
  • AI audio: Use AI music generators, TTS, synthetic sounds
  • Keep duration reasonable (15-60 seconds)
  • Similar volume levels for fair comparison

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
🎬 VIDEO CONTENT (Required: 10 pairs)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Location: public/content/videos/

Files needed:
  • human_1.mp4 to human_10.mp4
  • ai_1.mp4 to ai_10.mp4

Supported formats: .mp4, .webm

Tips for video content:
  • Human videos: Real footage, animations, film clips
  • AI videos: Use Runway, Pika, or other AI video generators
  • Keep short (10-30 seconds recommended)
  • Compress for web to keep file sizes manageable

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
⚙️ CUSTOMIZATION OPTIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Edit Question Descriptions:
   File: src/data/questions.json
   Change the "description" field for each question

2. Randomize Answer Positions:
   In questions.json, set "humanPosition" to "left" or "right"
   Mix it up so the answer isn't always in the same position!

3. Add More Content:
   You can extend beyond 10 pairs by adding more entries to
   questions.json (must increment IDs and update file paths)
